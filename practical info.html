Travel:

From Vancouver International Airport (YVR) to UBC Campus

Vancouver offers many transportation services, such as traditional taxi services, Uber and Lyft, car rental or car-sharing services such as Evo or Zipcar, and public transportation.

A traditional taxi or Uber from YVR to UBC typically costs $40 - $50 and takes approximately 30 minutes. Using public transportation will cost you approximately $10 but will take about 60 minutes.

To plan your travel efficiently while exploring Vancouver, we highly recommend using Google Maps.
